Here is a perl code example which shows progress of dd made by my friend <a href="http://www.dreier.nu/">Thor Dreier</a>:

    sdd if=/boot.img.gz of=/dev/sda -pg bs=1M 2>&1 | perl -e 'print $i++." MB\n" while(read(STDIN,$c,3));'
